Fourth Year Project

My fourth year project is based around the General Data Protetcion Regulation. The GDPR is a new regulation which is coming into affect in May 2018.

So far I have researched the area of data governance and the GDPR, and found out all there is to know from a customers and organizations prespective regarding the GDPR.

The reason this new regualation is coming into affect is to gives customers more rights regarding data that organizations process about them.

Organizations who fail to comply with the regulation will face harsh penalties so organizations are currently busy tyring to make sure they will be compliant when the regulation comes around in May 2018.

A big apsect of the GDPR is the security aspect, so the next phase of my project will look at the security of private databases and public databases and amke recommedataions to organizations on which I feel its the most secure way to store thier data. I feel this is important and worth purusing as the penalites for data breaches will be severe so organizations will need to secure their data in the best way possible.

I have decided to base my project around this area as it is an area I have a keen intrest in and as well as it being an important area for organizations, I am also looking forward to upskilling in the cloud computing area.


Third Year Project

During my internship in the Bank of Ireland I was required to complete a project related to my department. I decided to base my project around the Service Desk in the Bank Of Ireland.

I came to this decision as I felt the Service Desk is was an area in which I may gain future employment and also felt that I might be able to make a recommendation to the Bank regarding steps they should take to imporve their Service Desk model.

I first researched the Service Desk model used by the Bank and the different Service Desk models available.

I then interviewed many people across the organization from people who work on the Service Desk, staff who deal with the Service Desk, and customers who deal with the Service Desk.

After carrying out all these steps I was then in a position to make a recommendation to the Bank on what I felt was the best course of action they should take regarding their Service Desk.
